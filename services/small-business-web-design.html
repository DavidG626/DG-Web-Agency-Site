Why It Matters

Why Hand-Coded Beats a Template

Template websites share code with millions of other sites. They load slowly, break often, and look like everyone else. A hand-coded site is built specifically for your business. It loads faster, ranks higher on Google, and gives your customers a better experience.

Speed matters. Google uses page speed as a ranking factor. A faster site means better rankings, more clicks, and more customers.

Do I need to know anything technical to get a website?

Yes. Every site is built mobile-first and tested across devices. Most of your customers will find you on their phone, so your site needs to look great and load fast on any screen.

I can rebuild it from scratch. I will use your existing content, improve the design, and handle the migration so nothing breaks. You keep your domain and your search rankings carry over.
