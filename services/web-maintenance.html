Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What does website maintenance include?

Hosting, SSL certificate, security monitoring, content updates, and support. The exact inclusions depend on your plan. Everything your site needs to stay live, fast, and secure.

Is website maintenance included in the Website Care Plan?

Yes. Maintenance is not an add-on. It is included in every Website Care Plan along with your custom website, hosting, SEO, and support. There are no extra fees.

What happens if my website goes down?

I monitor your site and respond quickly to any issues. If something breaks, call or text me directly and I will fix it. You are not submitting a support ticket to a faceless company. You are calling the person who built your site.

How do I request content updates to my website?

Call, text, or email me with what you need changed. Content updates are included in every plan. The Starter plan includes 1 small update per month, the Business plan includes 2. Updates cover text changes, photo swaps, and minor adjustments. New pages or features are quoted separately.

Do I need to install updates or deal with plugins?

No. Your site is hand-coded, not built on WordPress or a CMS. There are no plugins to update, no themes to maintain, and no security patches to worry about. The site is lightweight, fast, and stable by design.
