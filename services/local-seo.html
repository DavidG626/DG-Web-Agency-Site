Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is local SEO and why does my business need it?

Local SEO is the process of optimizing your online presence so your business shows up when people in your area search for your services on Google. Without it, potential customers find your competitors instead of you.

How long does it take to see results from local SEO?

Most businesses start seeing improvements within 4 to 12 weeks. SEO is a long-term strategy. Rankings build over time as Google recognizes your site as relevant and trustworthy for your target keywords.

Do you guarantee first page rankings on Google?

No one can guarantee specific rankings. Google controls their algorithm. What I can guarantee is that your site will be built with SEO best practices, proper schema markup, fast load times, and optimized content that gives you the best chance of ranking.

Is local SEO included in the Website Care Plan?

Yes. Local SEO setup and ongoing improvements are included in every Website Care Plan. There is no extra charge for SEO. It is part of every website I build.

What is the difference between local SEO and regular SEO?

Local SEO focuses on helping your business appear in location-based searches, like "plumber near me" or "best bakery in Pasadena." Regular SEO targets broader, non-geographic keywords. For small businesses that serve a specific area, local SEO delivers the most relevant traffic.
