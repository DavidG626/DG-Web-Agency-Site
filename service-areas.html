Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to be local to work with you?

No. I work remotely with every client. We communicate by phone, text, and email. You never need to meet in person for me to build, launch, and manage your website.

Will my website be optimized for my specific city?

Yes. Every site I build includes local SEO targeting your city and service area. Your business name, address, and service keywords are built into the site structure, schema markup, and content so Google knows where you operate.

Do you have an office in my area?

I am based in Pasadena, California. I do not have physical offices in other regions. I work remotely and provide the same level of service to every client regardless of location.

What if I need changes or have questions?

Call or text me directly. I am one person, not a call center. You get the same developer who built your site every time you reach out.
